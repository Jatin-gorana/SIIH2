About Us

The Ministry of Jal Shakti is committed to improving water management practices, ensuring the sustainable use of groundwater resources, and addressing water-related issues across India. Our initiatives include monitoring water quality, promoting water conservation, and empowering communities to manage their water resources efficiently.

Our Services

Water Conservation

Our water conservation programs are designed to help communities adopt sustainable water management practices and conserve precious resources for future generations.

Groundwater Monitoring

We monitor groundwater levels and quality to ensure safe and sustainable use of this critical resource.

Water Quality Testing

We provide testing services to assess the quality of drinking water, ensuring safe and clean water for all.
